MY EPIC BIOGRAPHY
by Emilio James V. Perez
18 to 21 years old

At this point of my life, I am finally emerging as an adult, dito nagsimula ang aking college life. Madami akong pangarap, madami akong gusto magawa. I was really excited back then kasi you know nagiging isang adult na ako,, FREEDOOOMMMMM!!.

(Reaction ko nung nalaman ko na pwede nako makulong)

During those 3 years, dami kong pinagdaanan, I made alot of friends, I also made alot of enemies. Most of them suddenly cuts contact with you, some of them, you'll never see again. But part of growing up is realizing that every person you meet is just a passerby in your long long life. and only few sticks with you till the very end.
